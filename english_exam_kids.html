Read the stories and answer the questions

Story 1 — My Best Friend Max 🐕
My name is Lucy. I have a dog named Max. Max is big and brown. He has a long tail and big ears. Max loves to run and play in the garden. Every morning, Max wakes me up by licking my face! He is my best friend. After school, we always go for a walk together. Max is very friendly and he never barks at strangers. My mom says Max is the best dog in the world, and I agree!

Story 2 — A Trip to Space 🚀
Last night, I had an amazing dream. I was an astronaut! I put on my big white spacesuit and flew to the moon. The moon was very quiet and there was no air to breathe. I could see Earth from far away — it looked like a beautiful blue and green ball. I also saw millions of stars. They were everywhere! When I woke up, I decided that I want to be an astronaut when I grow up. Space is the most exciting place in the universe!

Listening Fun!

Listen and answer the questions below

🎵 A Day at the Zoo
Short dialogue · About 40 seconds
Teacher: Hi class! Today we're going to talk about a trip to the zoo. Sam, did you like the zoo?
Sam: Yes! It was amazing! I saw lions, giraffes, and monkeys!
Teacher: Wonderful! What was your favorite animal?
Sam: My favorite was the giraffe because it has a very long neck!
Teacher: And what did you eat there?
Sam: I ate an ice cream and my mom had a sandwich. It was delicious!
Teacher: Great! Did you take any photos?
Sam: Yes, I took lots of photos with my dad's phone!
L1
What animals did Sam see at the zoo? 🦁
L2
Why is the giraffe Sam's favorite animal? 🦒
L3
What did Sam eat at the zoo? 🍦
L4
✍️ What is YOUR favorite animal? Write 1-2 sentences!
